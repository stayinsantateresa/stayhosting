Our Story

We built this
on purpose.

Stay Hosting wasn't built to be another property manager. It was built because we believe the places people travel to for real experiences deserve better operations.

We started with a simple observation: the most compelling short-term rental markets — tropical coastlines, mountain towns, places where nature is the attraction — were being managed like commodity products. Generic listings. Static pricing. Absentee owners getting generic reports once a month.

We operate across two countries not because it's complicated, but because it makes us better. Our Costa Rica team is on the ground, living in the communities we work in. Our Argentina team handles the data-heavy work: revenue optimization, guest communication, analytics. Together, we built something that neither could do alone.

We choose our destinations carefully. From tropical beaches and rainforest on the Nicoya Peninsula to the lakes and mountains of Patagonia — we only operate in places people actually want to be. That's not marketing. It's strategy.

🌿

Nature-First Destinations

We intentionally choose small, high-potential destinations where lifestyle is the draw. Not mass tourism. Not cities.

📈

Performance-Driven

🤝

Owners as Partners

We treat every property like we own it. Because when we perform, everyone wins — and we intend to keep winning.
